## The Problem

> **46% of abdominal thrusts fail** due to incorrect technique.  
> Certificates prove attendance, not competence.

Current first aid training:
- Teaches *procedures* but doesn't verify *execution*
- Relies on instructor observation (subjective)
- Produces certificates that mean nothing in an emergency

When someone chokes, people freeze—even "trained" bystanders.

---

## Our Position

We are **not** a hardware company selling vests.  
We are **not** a training company competing with BCLS providers.

**We are a verification layer.**

We provide objective, auditable proof that trained individuals can actually perform choking rescue correctly.

---

## What We Sell

| Layer | Component | Value |
|:------|:----------|:------|
| **Hardware** | Sensor-equipped training vest | Captures force, angle, position |
| **Software** | Real-time AI coaching app | Immediate corrective feedback |
| **Data** | Competency reports (PDF) | Audit trail for liability protection |

The vest collects data. The app trains. The report **proves**.
